## Key Concepts
- Capital works (Division 43): Generally 2.5% p.a. over 40 years on eligible construction costs.
- Plant & equipment (Division 40): Depreciable assets like carpets, appliances ‚Äî subject to acquisition date rules.
- New vs established: New builds often have larger claim pools; established can still claim capital works and new items added post‚Äëpurchase.
- Quantity surveyor reports: Often required to substantiate claims; update after substantial renovations.
