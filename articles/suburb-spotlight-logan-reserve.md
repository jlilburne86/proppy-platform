# Suburb Spotlight: Logan Reserve

Infrastructure upgrades, population growth and relative affordability have drawn attention to this corridor. Here’s the data investors should weigh — and the risks to watch.

![Signals view](/assets/screenshots/technology.png)
![Compare nearby suburbs](/assets/screenshots/how-it-works.png)
![Pipeline & portfolio exposure](/assets/screenshots/homepage-9.png)

## Market Context
- Part of the broader SEQ growth story; rental competition remains firm in select pockets.

## Key Drivers
- New infrastructure spend, employment access, and net migration.

## What the Data Shows
- Vacancy tighter than surrounding areas in parts of the corridor; check dwelling mix and new estates pipeline.

## Expert Insight
- Focus on land/house specs, not just postcode; micro‑location and build quality drive outcomes.

## Investor Lens
- Beginners: verify rental appraisals and days‑on‑market; avoid overpaying for inclusions.
- Experienced: compare builder quality, covenant restrictions and street‑level nuances.

## Risks & Considerations
- Concentrated new supply risk, builder solvency, and flood/overlays. Validate all due diligence.

## Key Takeaways
- Let fundamentals (vacancy, demand drivers, build quality) lead; avoid FOMO.
- Diversity across corridors to reduce concentration risk.
