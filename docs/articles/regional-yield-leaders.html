Regional yield leaders: where rents work hardest

Proppy Editorial • Updated 28 Jan 2026

Outside the capitals, mining and regional hubs dominate top rental returns. High yields often come with higher volatility and single‑industry risk — validate tenant demand and exit liquidity before you buy.
