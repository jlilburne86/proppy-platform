Case Study: Multi‑City Portfolio Building Over 8 Years

Summary: A measured approach to buying across cycles: stabilisers first, accelerators when signals align, and strict quality filters.

Key Takeaways

  • City rotation is a tool, not a rule; insist on local signal confirmation.
  • Selection discipline compounds: orientation, layout usability, and low ongoing capex.
  • Periodic consolidation and debt recycling can reset buffers.

Approach

1) Baseline stabiliser in a tight rental micro‑market.

2) Accelerator when listings tighten and DOM falls in a constrained corridor.

3) Rebalance; improve or sell B‑grade to upgrade quality.

Risks & Controls

  • Over‑concentration risk → spread by corridor and stock type, not just state.
  • Cashflow strain → keep buffers, refinance only when prudent.
