How the Guarantee Works

We keep it simple. No hidden loopholes, just a straightforward commitment to finding you the right property.

1

Define the Brief

We work with you to establish clear criteria: yield, capital growth potential, location parameters, and budget. This becomes our "agreed search mandate".

2

90-Day Search

Our team uses proprietary data and off-market networks to hunt for properties that match your specific mandate. We present you with vetted options.

3

Deliver or Refund

If we fail to present a property that meets the agreed criteria within 90 days, you are entitled to a full refund of your retainer fee. No questions asked.
